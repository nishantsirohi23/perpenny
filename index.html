Refund Policy

At PerPenny Works LLP, customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not satisfied with the service provided, you may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

Eligibility for Refund

  • A refund request must be submitted within 24 hours of the service completion.
  • Refunds are applicable if the service provided is significantly different from the description or agreed-upon terms.
  • Refunds will be considered if the service provider fails to complete the task or if there are substantial issues with the service quality.

How to Request a Refund

  • To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at perpennyworksllp@gmail.com with the details of your service and the reason for your refund request.
  • Provide any relevant evidence or documentation to support your claim, such as photographs or service receipts.

Refund Process

  • Upon receiving your refund request, our team will review the details and determine eligibility.
  • If approved, the refund will be processed within 5 working days from the date of approval.
  • The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the transaction.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Refunds will not be issued for services that have been completed to the agreed-upon standards.
  • Refunds are not applicable if the service was canceled by the customer without a valid reason.
  • Any disputes arising after 24 hours of service completion will not be eligible for a refund.
  • If food or items are delivered or packed, cancellations and refunds are not applicable.

For any questions or further assistance regarding our refund policy, please contact our customer support team at perpennyworksllp@gmail.com or call us at +91 8630277749.
